    • ABOUT US

    The vision for our Gospel FELLOWSHIP was born from a deep hunger for authentic Christain community rooted in the truth of God's word and the power of the holy spirit. in a time when many were searching for meaning,connection,and spiritual depth, a few believers began to gather with a shared conviction: to return to the simplicity and strength of the early church-devoted to the apostles' teaching, to the studying of the word(HOLY BIBLE), and to prayer. What began as a small prayer group in living rooms soon grew into a vibrant fellowship centered on the gospel of Jesus Christ, The foundation is clear:JESUS IS THE ONLY WAY,THE TRUTH,AND THE LIFE(John 14:6), and the message of his death and burial,and ressurection is the power of God unto salvation for everyone who believes (Romans 1:16).

    We welcome you with great joy to THE GYN FELLOWSHIP. We are here to share the great news of Jesus Christ to all that will listen.
